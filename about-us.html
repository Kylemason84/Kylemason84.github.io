Cast Iron Coffee was opened Spring of 2020 with the goal of providing good coffee to DeKalb, IL and Northern Illinois University. We look forward to serving you quality drinks in a warm, inviting atmosphere for many years to come. Our hope is that you would feel at home here at Cast Iron Coffee whether you are a coffee expert, or just just learning the ropes.
